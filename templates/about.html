About Job Assistant

Job Assistant is an AI-powered recruitment platform that helps job seekers build ATS-friendly resumes, improve resume quality, analyze job compatibility, and discover the best career opportunities through intelligent recommendations.

AI ATS Analyzer

Analyze resumes against job descriptions using Artificial Intelligence. Receive ATS scores, keyword matching, and personalized suggestions.

Resume Builder

Create professional resumes from scratch with modern templates and download them instantly.

Resume Improvement

Improve your existing resume with AI-powered recommendations to increase interview chances.

Job Recommendation

Receive personalized job recommendations based on skills, education, and experience.

Our Mission

Our mission is to bridge the gap between talented candidates and recruiters using Artificial Intelligence. We help users prepare ATS-friendly resumes, identify missing skills, and confidently apply for jobs.

Our Vision

To become one of the leading AI-powered recruitment platforms by providing smart resume analysis, career guidance, and intelligent hiring solutions for both job seekers and employers.

Platform Highlights

  • AI Resume Analysis — Instant ATS score and keyword matching.

  • Job Portal — Browse verified jobs approved by Super Admin.

  • Modern Dashboard — Responsive interface with a professional design.
