Review the following information to use the CareerMomentum platform effectively.

🤖 AI-Powered Job Assistant

  • ATS Scoring: Instantly check how well your resume matches a job description.
  • Resume Tools: Use our tools to improve an existing resume or build a new one.
  • Application Flow: Move from job search to application submission in a simple flow.

🏢 Admin & Super Admin Guidelines

  • Job Lifecycle: Admins can create job posts and submit them for approval.
  • Quality Assurance: Jobs are published only after Super Admin review.
  • Monitoring: Super Admins can monitor the total number of users on the platform.

📋 How it Works

  • User: Log in, upload your resume, improve your ATS score, and apply for jobs.
  • Admin: Log in, create job posts, and check approval status.
  • Super Admin: Review job posts and approve or reject them.
