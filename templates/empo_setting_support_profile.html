Frequently Asked Questions

How do I post a new job? expand_more
Go to the Jobs tab, fill in the job details form, and click Post Job. It appears instantly in your active postings.
Why don't I see any candidates? expand_more
The Candidates page only shows applicants for jobs you've posted. Post a job first to start receiving applicants.
How is the match score calculated? expand_more
Lumina AI compares a candidate's skills and experience against your job description and key skills to generate a match percentage.
