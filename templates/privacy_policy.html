Last Updated: July 2026

1. Introduction

Job Assistant respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information while using our platform.

2. Information We Collect

  • Name
  • Email Address
  • Resume (PDF / DOCX)
  • Skills and Education Details
  • Job Application Information
  • Login Information

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Analyze resumes using AI.
  • Generate ATS scores.
  • Recommend suitable jobs.
  • Improve user experience.
  • Maintain platform security.

4. Data Protection

We use industry-standard security practices to protect your personal data. Only authorized administrators can access stored information.

5. Resume Privacy

Uploaded resumes are used only for ATS analysis, resume improvement, and job recommendation. They are never shared without your permission.

6. Cookies

Our platform may use cookies to improve performance, remember preferences, and enhance your browsing experience.

7. Third-Party Services

Job Assistant may integrate trusted AI services and email services to provide resume analysis and notifications.

8. User Rights

  • Access your personal information.
  • Update profile details.
  • Delete your account.
  • Request removal of uploaded resumes.

9. Contact Us

Email: support@jobassistant.com

For any privacy-related questions, feel free to contact our support team.
