Guides & how-tos

quizFrequently Asked Questions

How is my ATS score calculated?

We compare your resume's keywords, formatting, and structure against the job description to estimate how well it would pass an applicant tracking system.

Can recruiters see my saved jobs?

No. Saved jobs are private and only visible to you until you actually apply.

How do I update the resume used for matching?

Go to your Profile and upload a new resume — your skills and contact info are refreshed automatically.

How do I delete my account?

Head to Settings → Account → Danger Zone. This permanently removes your data and can't be undone.
